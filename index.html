Dr. Hemachandra Tokala

Consultant Cardiothoracic & Vascular Surgeon
M.B.B.S • D.N.B FIAGES M.Ch (CTS-NIMS) Former – Assistant Professor SIVMS & SVRRGGH

Dr. Hemachandra Tokala is Tirupati's foremost specialist in cardiothoracic and vascular surgery. He completed his M.Ch (CTS-NIMS) — Cardiothoracic & Vascular Surgery — from NIMS, Hyderabad, one of India's premier surgical institutions.

Holding M.B.B.S, D.N.B and FIAGES, Dr. Tokala served as Former Assistant Professor at SIVMS and SVRRGGH — bringing academic excellence and research rigour to every procedure.

His philosophy: Trust, Care, and Precision — delivering world-class, personalised surgical outcomes to every patient across Andhra Pradesh.
